# Sales-Analytics
Sales analytics project focuses on how customers interact with retails product across differents locations. By analyzing retail transactional data, the project identifies key customer orders, sales performance, and product affinities. these insights help businesses make informed decisions about marketing and customer retention strategies.

## Project Objectives
The project aims to:
- Analyze sales distrubtion by category, sub-category amd product performance
- Study customer purchase behavior base on quantity order, state amd city
- Evaluate monthly/annuanly sales and profit trends and identify seasonal appaterns
- compare high profit vs low profit category
- Identify the most preferred payment methods.

## Data Preparation & Cleaning
- Handle missing value, inconsistencies and data types
- Convert dates into proper format and extract and time feature ( month, year, weeks and quarters)
- create new calculated columns (first purchase date and profit margin)

## Key Performance Indicator (KPI)
- **Total Orders** : **1194**
- **Revenue Generated** : **$7,793,336**
- **Total Profit** : **$1,610,697**
- **Average Profit Margin** : **19.66%**
- **Total Cost of Goods** : **$6,182,639**
- **Total Quantity Sold** : **12,745**

## Insight Generation & Recommendation
1. **Trend Insight**
   - Insight: Sales consistently grow from 2021 to 2024 but dropped in early 2025, Quarter 4 shows the highest sales every year indicating strong seasonal demand
   - Recommendation: Increase inventory amd marketing in Q4 to maximize seasonaal revenue.
   
2. **Profitability Insight**
   - Insight: Office supplier has high sales (33.89%) but recorded low profit margin (19.54%)
   - Recommendation: Adjusting pricing, renegotiate supplier costs.
   - Insight: furniture have both high sales (33.1%) and high profit margin (19.80%), indicating optimal profitability
   - Recommendation: Increase stocks, run promotions and bundle high margin product
   - insight : Electronics maintain a healthy profit margin (19.65%) and sales (33.02%)
   - Recommendation : Boost electronics demand through targeted campaigns
